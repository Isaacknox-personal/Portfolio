About Me

I’m a student at Tech Valley High School with a strong interest in mechanical systems, outdoor education, and hands-on problem-solving.

I’m currently a PSIA–AASI certified ski, snowboard, and telemark instructor at Stratton Mountain Resort, where I’ve spent the past three years developing both technical and leadership skills in teaching, collaboration, and safety.

Outside of the resort, I work as a Shell Technician and team member at the Albany Rowing Center, maintaining rowing shells and equipment while continuing to train and compete.

I’m also certified in lifeguarding and Basic Life Support (BLS) through the American Red Cross, reflecting my commitment to safety and responsibility in every environment.
